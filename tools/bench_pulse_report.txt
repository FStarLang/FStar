================================================================================
  KEY FINDINGS
================================================================================

1. Auto-pattern inference works well for MOST code — zero regressions
   in tests/ and examples/ after targeted {:nopattern} fixes.

2. The main challenge is the DEPENDENCY CHAIN: when auto-patterns are
   baked into .checked files, they propagate to ALL downstream consumers.
   Patterns inferred on fundamental definitions like Prims.subtype_of
   or FStar.Pervasives WP definitions have outsized impact.

3. Core WP definitions (Pure, Ghost effect WPs) and type-level
   definitions (subtype_of, prop) MUST use {:nopattern} to prevent
   cascading regressions.

4. Pulse builds ~10% faster with auto_patterns (2:03 vs 2:18),
   with 2 remaining PulseCore failures that need manual proof fixes.
